About Bridgit

Bridgit Mendler is a singer, songwriter and actress. She’s best known for her role in the Disney Channel show ‘Good Luck Charlie’ where she played Teddy Duncan, a teenager who recorded videos of her life with advice for her baby sister, Charlie, to watch in the future. She was also featured in the NBC show ‘Undateable’, the Netflix movie ‘Merry Happy Whatever’ and a Disney Channel Original Movie, ‘Lemonade Mouth.’ Bridgit has also pursued a music career alongside her acting career. Her debut album was titled ‘Hello My Name Is…’ and included hits such as ‘Ready Or Not’ and ‘Determinate.’

In 2020, she graduated from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) Media Lab as a Ph.D. student. She is also the niece of Dr. Christine Blasey Ford, who testified in front of the Senate Judiciary Committee about her allegations of sexual assault against Brett Kavanaugh.
